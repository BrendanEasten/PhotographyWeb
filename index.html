Why Photography?

  1. Capturing Moments: I get to freeze beautiful moments and turn them into lasting memories
  2. Creative Expression: I get to use light and composition to share my unique perspective
  3. Connection with the World: I get to notice and highlight the beauty that others might miss

Personal Tips

  • Focus on Lighting: Natural light is key, so adjust to it.
  • Use the Rule of Thirds: Align key elements with a 3x3 grid.
  • Keep It Steady: Keep the camera steady to avoid blur.
  • Have A Creative Eye: Keep an eye out on any beautiful landscapes or imagery
